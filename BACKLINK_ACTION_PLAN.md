## 🥉 Tier 3 — Editorial & outreach (higher value, more effort)
Real authority comes from editorial links. These move the needle most for US/UK.

1. **Get into the listicles that already rank.** From live research, these rank for your exact terms — email the authors to be added:
   - "Top WooCommerce development agencies" (e.g., helloroketto.com)
   - "Best offshore development companies in India" (e.g., thescalers.com blog)
   - Pitch: 1 short paragraph + your Freelancer 978-review proof. High relevance, geo-targeted.
2. **HARO / Connectively / Featured.com / Qwoted** — answer journalist queries on web-dev, outsourcing, hiring developers → earns links from real US/UK news sites. 2–3 answers/week.
3. **Republish your `/resources/` articles** on **Medium, Dev.to, Hashnode** with a canonical tag + link back. Dev community is US-heavy = geo authority.
4. **Guest posts** on SaaS/agency/dev blogs that accept contributors (1–2/month).
